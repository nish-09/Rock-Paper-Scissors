How to Play

This is a "Best of 10" series. The first player to reach a score of 10 wins the game!

Rules:

  • Rock (✊) beats Scissors (✌️)
  • Scissors (✌️) beats Paper (✋)
  • Paper (✋) beats Rock (✊)
  • If both players choose the same item, it's a tie! In this version, both players will receive one point in the event of a tie.
